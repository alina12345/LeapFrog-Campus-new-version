Welcome

Leapfrog Campus was established in 2006 A.D. under the affiliation of Tribhuvan University,on the chairmanship of Mr.Richan Shrestha with the aim of providing all-round education to the students. Along with high academic standards, we strive to make Leapfrog Campus a place for students to develop life skills and nurture their talents and abilities to become all-rounded citizen of the future.

Message from the Principal

It has been a matter of immense pleasure for me on mentioning that Leapfrog Campus has completed six glorious years of success and has proved to be the leading educational institution across the nation. Our vision is to promote four pillars of education which stand out for excellence.
