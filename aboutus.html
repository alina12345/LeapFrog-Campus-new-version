      What we do

We promote excellence and equity in education through courses in Engineering for Bachelors, and by providing students a path to college opportunities, including financial support and scholarships in Master programs. We also serve the education community through research and advocacy on behalf of students, educators, schools and colleges.

Beyond that we are collaborating with fifty plus universities abroad. We often organize Lecturers and students exchange programs so that we can better understand our collaborators and on top of that we believe that we are training our faculties and students to learn about latest technology and adopt them to fulfill the demands of the globe. Our effort is to broaden the knowledge and skill of our faculties and students in diversified environment to serve the mankind.

      Whom we serve

Through our work we serve more than fifteen hundred students each year enrolled in various programs in Bachelors in Engineering. Our aim is to produce engineers who are highly qualified and at the same time are ready to serve the mankind and can come up with innovative ideas which facilitates the modern techno-trends and at the same time conserve the world for future.

      History

In 2006 AD Leapfrog Campus was established as an Engineering College under the affiliation of Tribhuwan university in the premises of Kupondole, Lalitpur. The initiation of establishing this college was taken by Mr. Himal Karmacharya. We started with 70 students (20 students each in BE Civil, BE Computer and BE Electrical & Electronics). Today thousands of students compete in the entrance exam for 300 seats in each faculties (BE Civil, BE Computer, BArc, BE Electronics and BE IT). We were overwhelmed when our college won the Best Engineering College Award 2012 given by Ministry of Education last year. Till now three batch of students have graduated from here, which totals to One Hundred and Twenty Engineers. We are proud that since 2009 we are in the top of the list of Best Engineering Colleges of Nepal.
